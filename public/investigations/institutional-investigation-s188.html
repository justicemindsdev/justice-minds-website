Investigation into Section 188 Housing Act 1996 Breach: Westminster City Council

Case Reference: WCC/S188/2024-001
Investigation Period: November 2024 - October 2025
Lead Investigator: Justice Minds Forensic Intelligence Limited
Evidence Items: 286+ documented communications
Report Date: 17 October 2025
Classification: Public Record

Executive Summary

This forensic investigation documents Westminster City Council's systematic breach of Section 188 Housing Act 1996 statutory duty to provide interim emergency accommodation. The investigation analyzed 286 documented communications across 10 months, involving multiple officers and contracted agencies.

Primary Finding: Every documented contact demonstrates clear acknowledgment of homelessness crisis under Section 188 "reason to believe" threshold. Despite comprehensive institutional knowledge spanning three departments, no emergency interim housing was provided at any point during the 10-month period under investigation.
